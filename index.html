Generative Emotive Sounds for Robots

As an essential part of the emotive exchange, Non- linguistic utterances (NLUs) play a big role in the interactions not only between humans, but also between humans and robots. This research seeks to further understand in a quantitative manner the role of NLUs in human-robot emotive exchange, and to investigate the connections between certain audio quali- ties, and perceived emotional arousal and pleasure.
